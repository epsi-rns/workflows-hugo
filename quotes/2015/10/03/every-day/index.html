I've been thinking about this over and over and over.

I mean, really, truly, imagine it.

What does that look like in a year? In 10 years?

What will happen to your life?

How alone will it make you?

How much is that gonna cut you off from your family?

How much is that gonna cut you off from everyone you know?

How will it feel for you, almost entirely alone, and not be able to explain to anyone?
